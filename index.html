Research Question

When was mis/disinformation about Aquino's involvement in the death of SAF 44 most prevalent?

Hypothesis

Mis/disinformation about Aquino's involvement in the death of SAF 44 was most prevalent during the time of the national elections.

Null Hypothesis

The timing of the national elections has no correlation with the spread of mis/disinformation about Aquino's involvement in the death of SAF 44.

Action Plan

Analyze the dates and frequency of mis/disinformation tweets about Aquino's involvement in the death of SAF 44.
